#+title: Guinevere Lavigny
#+filetags: :3308:3309:Empire:Individual:

Countess Guinevere Lavigny is a member of the Imperial Family and a
cousin of Emperor [[id:34f3cfdd-0536-40a9-8732-13bf3a5e4a70][Arissa Lavigny-Duval]]. GalNet described her as a niece
of [[id:33f63de9-fd79-4790-a1a5-ebd87aaeea2d][Florence Lavigny]] and the sister of Admiral Lucius Lavigny.

In 3308 and 3309 she was among the Duval relatives discussed in reports
about increased security for Imperial households and Florence
Lavigny's illness.
