* 0031 Icy Rings
[[id:77f6018d-79f1-4561-a607-39476d33670e][Flyai Gruia RN-K c11-7]]

Commander Newman1702 has stated that this is one of the most beautiful locations in the galaxy. This tiny icy moon orbits a ringed gas giant while almost touching its rings, providing some incredible views from the surface. There's also an [[id:7fec1142-ab20-47e1-b746-36edee889cc3][Earth-like]] world in the system to admire.
