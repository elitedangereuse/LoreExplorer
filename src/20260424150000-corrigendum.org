#+title: Corrigendum
#+filetags: :3301:3302:Player:Individual:

Corrigendum is a Pilots Federation commander who was frequently quoted
in 3301 and 3302 regarding Imperial politics, anti-piracy operations and
Canonn-related disputes. Reports variously identified him with
Lavigny's Legion and Loren's Legion.
