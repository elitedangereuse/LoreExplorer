* 0033 Size Matters
[[id:56376d0b-5ca2-4296-ad83-890b3d83f7d8][Gacrux]]

Gacrux, also known as Gamma Crucis, is one of the bigger stars in the galaxy and the nearest red giant star to the [[id:6ace5ab9-af2a-4ad7-bb52-6059c0d3ab4a][Sol]] system. Commander AreZee informs travellers that despite the orbital distance of Hopkinson's Inheritance, the star is clearly visible from the surface port and is a sublime spectacle.
