* Community Goal: Bringing Back the Bourbon

/14 Apr 3302/

Rumours from the [[id:d40886f3-bc59-4f0d-8926-b125ee01c9bb][Epsilon Indi]] system suggest that the United [[id:d40886f3-bc59-4f0d-8926-b125ee01c9bb][Epsilon Indi]] Flag is recruiting independent Commanders for a campaign called 'Bribes 4 Bourbon'. In a series of illicit meetings with system officials, the organisation reportedly secured an agreement to revise the status of [[id:ffcc8c23-1721-41cb-9cb7-c849292c8b38][Indi Bourbon]] and make it a legal beverage.

Yulia 'The Needle' Kuriakova, a spokeswoman for the organisation, said:

"For almost a year, authorities in [[id:d40886f3-bc59-4f0d-8926-b125ee01c9bb][Epsilon Indi]] have prohibited the production of [[id:ffcc8c23-1721-41cb-9cb7-c849292c8b38][Indi Bourbon]], which is classified as an illegal substance. Our plan is to bring vast quantities of illegal liquor [[id:70fecaa7-bd23-4579-b206-7e3e65737044][Mansfield Orbiter]] to pay off the officials."

The United [[id:d40886f3-bc59-4f0d-8926-b125ee01c9bb][Epsilon Indi]] Flag has requested that [[id:221834bd-28f1-4d70-af5b-3d8558884da0][Burnham Bile Distillate]], [[id:f38c2905-34c5-45c1-a2f5-2ec352b6630f][Eranin]] Pearl Whisky, [[id:41fa0a3d-4f14-44c1-b314-78882dcc37b3][Leestian Evil Juice]] and [[id:eddbd97b-5374-4220-b6c0-26731c953c00][Wuthielo Ku Froth]] be smuggled into [[id:70fecaa7-bd23-4579-b206-7e3e65737044][Mansfield Orbiter]]. A temporary black market has been established to accept these commodities.

[[id:d56d0a6d-142a-4110-9c9a-235df02a99e0][The Federation]], meanwhile, has asserted that the campaign represents an act of economic malfeasance, and has accused the United [[id:d40886f3-bc59-4f0d-8926-b125ee01c9bb][Epsilon Indi]] Flag of inciting civil disobedience. But [[id:d40886f3-bc59-4f0d-8926-b125ee01c9bb][Epsilon Indi]] is no stranger to dissent: in November last year an explosive-smuggling operation organised by a group called Revolution Incorporated was uncovered.

The United [[id:d40886f3-bc59-4f0d-8926-b125ee01c9bb][Epsilon Indi]] Flag has promised to reward pilots who deliver rare liquors to [[id:70fecaa7-bd23-4579-b206-7e3e65737044][Mansfield Orbiter]] in the [[id:d40886f3-bc59-4f0d-8926-b125ee01c9bb][Epsilon Indi]] system. The campaign begins on the 14th of April 3302 and will run for one week. If the final target is met earlier than planned, the campaign will end immediately.
