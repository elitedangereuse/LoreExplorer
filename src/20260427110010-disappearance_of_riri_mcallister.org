#+title: Disappearance of Riri McAllister
#+filetags: :3304:Alliance:Empire:Federation:

After being shot during her trial, [[id:315a2182-7266-45f9-ac87-5f0f42c5cf12][Riri McAllister]] was transported
aboard a medical ship that later vanished and was presumed destroyed.
Officials listed McAllister, the [[id:f5c4ef7c-7a40-4d9f-87e1-904f11efda2f][League of Reparation]]'s coordinator
known as Nexus, as missing rather than definitively dead.
