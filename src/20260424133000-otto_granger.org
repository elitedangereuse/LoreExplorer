#+title: Otto Granger
#+filetags: :3302:Federation:Individual:

Otto Granger was a smuggler and bounty hunter associated with the
so-called Granger Gang. In 3302 he orchestrated a public clue hunt
around mysterious wreckage in the [[id:c32901ed-73d1-4ca6-aeb8-5bcd795d1036][Pleiades Sector AB-W B2-4]] system,
claiming to have made a discovery of profound importance.

GalNet later reported that Granger demanded credit for the find after
others were officially associated with the wreck’s discovery.
