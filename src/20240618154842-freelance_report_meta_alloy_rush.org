* Freelance Report: Meta-Alloy Rush

/18 Jul 3302/

As the [[id:d56d0a6d-142a-4110-9c9a-235df02a99e0][Federation]] and [[id:77cf2f14-105e-4041-af04-1213f3e7383c][Empire]] attempt to lay claim to the organic structures known as barnacles, the demand for meta-alloys has skyrocketed. Many starports have begun placing orders for the material as a hedge against potential malfunctions.

Vernon Trask, lead engineer at Bauschinger City, said: "Some stations have been defunct for nearly a year because of those corrosive Artefacts. We can't afford to be out of commission that long."

"Quite a few stations in the Empire have open orders for meta-alloys, including Bauschinger. We're hoping to get ahead of this techno-plague before it bites us."

This 'meta-alloy rush' has alarmed conservationists and researchers concerned about the future of the barnacles.

"We know so little about the barnacles," said Dr [[id:40249bad-6066-4b89-aa4c-ba66ed58470f][Elana Lorax]], a noted xenobiologist. "We don't know how they grow, how long they live or how they create meta-alloys. If the exploitation continues at this rate, we may never know."
