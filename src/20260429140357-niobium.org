Niobium, Nb, atomic number 41. Melting point 2750K. This elements is
often used in alloys, but its properties also make it useful for
super-alloys needed for high temperature and extreme pressure
applications. It's also used in electroceramic applications and
[[id:94637eec-3dd2-478f-9f38-8930b96a15fe][superconductors]].
