Commander Yannik suggests this system as a worthy tourist destination:

An odd system, with two Neutron Stars, a Black Hole, and two Class G stars. Each Neutron Star is paired with a Class G. With the Black Hole near one of the pairs. This system is 28000LY from [[id:6ace5ab9-af2a-4ad7-bb52-6059c0d3ab4a][Sol]], and only a few thousand LY from [[id:ba6c6359-137b-4f86-ad93-f8ae56b0ad34][Colonia]].
