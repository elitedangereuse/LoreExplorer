#+title: Beryllium
#+filetags: :Commodity:

#+begin_quote
Beryllium, Be, atomic number 4. Melting point 1560K. A white-grey
alkaline earth metal. Widely used in the spacecraft industry right
from its early pre-interstellar history because of its high thermal
stability and low density. It is also a very useful component of
many alloys, both industrially and for specialist high tech
components.
#+end_quote
