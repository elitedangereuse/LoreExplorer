#+title: Ad Pontes

Ad Pontes is a world in the [[id:7a442368-d9e1-420b-a2c4-a8900a94227d][39 Tauri]] system, promoted as "The Planet of the
Zodiac" and known for tourism, natural beauty, and twelve orbiting ice
moons.
