#+title: Operation Nehet
#+filetags: :3301:Empire:

Operation Nehet was a community initiative ordered by Princess
[[id:34f3cfdd-0536-40a9-8732-13bf3a5e4a70][Arissa Lavigny-Duval]] as part of the investigation into the attempted
assassination of Emperor [[id:3cb0755e-4deb-442b-898b-3f0c6651636e][Hengist Duval]]. Pilots targeted criminal
elements around the [[id:1b91efee-b411-45a9-8b03-df967281885d][Nehet]] Silver Ring, and later reporting linked the
Silver Allied Network to the plot.
