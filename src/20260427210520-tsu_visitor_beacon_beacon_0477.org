* 0477 Tsu Visitor Beacon
[[id:4e04c739-9af1-449f-8a8b-c2d254aecf55][Tsu]]

Forming part of a rare trinary planet system, this water world is one of two in this star system. As the warmer of the two, and with an argon atmosphere, it has evolved some peculiar forms of life.
