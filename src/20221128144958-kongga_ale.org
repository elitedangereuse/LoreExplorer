#+title: Kongga Ale
#+filetags: :Commodity:rare:

#+begin_quote
The hops of [[id:73823700-53f1-4ae2-bfd4-334f2dee64a9][Kongga]] provide a deep, satisfying flavour to the locally
brewed ale, but seem to wither and spoil rapidly when removed from
their native climate. Due to this, Kongga Ale is renowned for its
authentic flavour and traditional production methods. Served at room
temperature with a decorative umbrella.
#+end_quote

This rare good is legal in all systems except for Prison Colony systems
and Federal Theocracy systems.

Sold as a rare commodity at [[id:64e61a3f-ef02-4774-adab-741b526505d0][Laplace Ring]], [[id:73823700-53f1-4ae2-bfd4-334f2dee64a9][Kongga]].
