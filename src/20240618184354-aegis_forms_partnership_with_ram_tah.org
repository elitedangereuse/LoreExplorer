#+title: Aegis Forms Partnership with Ram Tah
#+filetags: :3305:galnet:

* Aegis Forms Partnership with [[id:4551539e-a6b2-4c45-8923-40fb603202b7][Ram Tah]]

/17 Apr 3305/

[[id:85d9e888-3f5b-40ed-b8af-2eb87e42b0d0][Aegis]] has entered into a partnership with the engineer [[id:4551539e-a6b2-4c45-8923-40fb603202b7][Ram Tah]] to manufacture Guardian-related technology.

Senior Engineer [[id:209d7dfc-8691-490e-ba48-7e0519172137][Lilith Galloway]] made the following statement on behalf of [[id:85d9e888-3f5b-40ed-b8af-2eb87e42b0d0][Aegis]]:

“We believe that [[id:4551539e-a6b2-4c45-8923-40fb603202b7][Ram Tah]]’s accomplishments, while impressive, represent only a fraction of what can be accomplished. We have therefore entered into an agreement that will allow [[id:85d9e888-3f5b-40ed-b8af-2eb87e42b0d0][Aegis]] to bring its considerable resources to bear in the manufacture of Guardian-related technology, both now and in the future.”

Galloway confirmed that technology brokers would remain licensed distributors of the Trident, Javelin and Lance XG fighters.

[[id:4551539e-a6b2-4c45-8923-40fb603202b7][Ram Tah]] also gave a short statement to the media:

“To me, [[id:85d9e888-3f5b-40ed-b8af-2eb87e42b0d0][Aegis]] represents what can be achieved when humanity pulls together, and I am delighted to be entering into this partnership.”
