#+title: Command Capital
#+filetags: :manual:Federation:Empire:Alliance:

Command Capital, usually abbreviated to CC, is the strategic resource
used in [[id:4ed9ac75-9ddc-4533-bc21-00a775fbc0ee][Powerplay]] to measure a power's ability to hold territory.
The players guides explain that powers spend CC to maintain
[[id:1aaf6f29-ef6a-430c-b4e4-f40cf903a7a9][Control System]]s, while collecting CC income from nearby
[[id:69d33069-1971-47bd-8aff-fd9d06dfcfc6][Exploited System]]s.

If a power starts a cycle with a CC deficit, the most expensive control
systems can fall into [[id:77774790-2861-4ffe-b8b9-6e75effa38a8][Turmoil]] and may eventually revolt.
