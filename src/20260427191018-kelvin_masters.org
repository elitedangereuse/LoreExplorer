#+title: Kelvin Masters
#+filetags: :Individual:

Kelvin Masters is a freelance journalist and commentator associated
with the [[id:d06803e0-267c-4ffc-88f2-967058fce82e][Rewired]] news feed. He has frequently offered analysis on the
[[id:09343513-2893-458e-a689-5865fdc32e0a][Thargoids]], the [[id:b41c0ea3-93f4-4aef-8c9c-db0b140719c0][barnacles]] and frontier developments such as the
[[id:6fee136e-381f-47a2-ba13-6d74c8798803][California Nebula]].
