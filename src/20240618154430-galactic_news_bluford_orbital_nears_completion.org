* Galactic News: Bluford Orbital Nears Completion

/10 Mar 3302/

[[id:951f5c68-2395-4eb9-af43-b26172d0fe64][LHS 3447]] Dynamic and Co has announced that the campaign to construct a new starport in the [[id:951f5c68-2395-4eb9-af43-b26172d0fe64][LHS 3447]] system has reached a successful conclusion. All the necessary materials for the starport have now been received, and the agitators disrupting delivery operations have been neutralised. A spokesperson for the Pilots Federation, which helped to organise the campaign, released a brief statement:

"Once again the galaxy's independent pilots have demonstrated their remarkable commitment and resolve. This new starport will facilitate travel and trade throughout [[id:951f5c68-2395-4eb9-af43-b26172d0fe64][LHS 3447]], and serve as a testament to the dedication and altruism of the galactic community."

[[id:64e5b644-cc28-4586-a4a4-32dc226447bd][Bluford Orbital]] is expected to open its doors in the next few days.
