#+begin_quote

first interstellar war. Since 2350, the Sirius Corporation has been
using the vast amounts of cheap energy available on Lucifer from
coils wrapped around the planet for the synthesis of custom elements
(particularly military-grade fuel). Has the ancient name of Alpha
Canis Majoris. The permit for this system can be obtained via the
Sirius Corporation faction.
