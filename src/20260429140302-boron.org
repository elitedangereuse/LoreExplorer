#+begin_quote
Collected from mining asteroids.

Boron is used in ship interior structures, insulation and in the
creation of polymers and ceramics used in station and ship
construction. Boron is also used in the manufacture of glass for
ships and stations, offering greater strength and thermal shock
resistance than other manufacturing techniques.

Other uses include agricultural medicine, insecticides and some
advanced medicines.
