#+title: Spinward Marches Alliance Concern
#+filetags: :Alliance:

The Spinward Marches Alliance Concern is an Alliance-aligned
organisation active in frontier aid, security and agricultural support.
It is associated with relief operations in [[id:0a64d5ad-8198-427c-b059-84a18e468c53][Morrina]] and supply efforts
supporting the [[id:6fee136e-381f-47a2-ba13-6d74c8798803][California Nebula]] enclave.
