* 0529 Cupid's Arrow
[[id:182672af-8d65-4d9e-b55f-76dacf81531c][S171 34]]

Commander Ray Mobula says this about this tourist destination: "[[id:182672af-8d65-4d9e-b55f-76dacf81531c][S171 34]], better known as the gate to the beautiful NGC 7822 Nebula, is a famous system among lovers and the premier place to spend a honeymoon. Why? The beautiful NGC 7822 Nebula is pierced by a line of O-stars - therefore it has been compared to Cupid's arrow piercing a heart. Approaching this nebula is an experience in its own, with each jump the bright band of O-stars becomes clearer and clearer as well as the Nebula unfolds its colourful beauty. Once arrived, this area is filled with black holes and vibrant stars. On top of this, a cruise to S171-34 provides the unique opportunity to take a stop in the Maia system and visit one of the nearby Barnacles."
