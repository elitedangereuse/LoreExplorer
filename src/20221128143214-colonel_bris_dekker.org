#+title: Colonel Bris Dekker
#+filetags: :Federation:Individual:engineer:

#+begin_quote
Colonel Bris Dekker retired with full honours a decade ago. He
served as a Federal Navy officer as part of an elite recon squadron
and has an almost natural affinity for frame shift technology. Even
though he is retired, the navy call on him to evaluate any changes
or upgrades. His facility has recently been vastly upgraded to
support the training courses that he provides as well as his regular
workshops. Develop your relationship with him to discover another
engineer.
#+end_quote
