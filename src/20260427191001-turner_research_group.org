#+title: Turner Research Group
#+filetags: :Alliance:

The Turner Research Group is a scientific organisation associated with
Alliance operations in the [[id:6fee136e-381f-47a2-ba13-6d74c8798803][California Nebula]]. It was established to
study the region's [[id:b41c0ea3-93f4-4aef-8c9c-db0b140719c0][barnacles]] and later became involved in logistics,
construction projects and local defence.
