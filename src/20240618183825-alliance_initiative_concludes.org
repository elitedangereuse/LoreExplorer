* Alliance Initiative Concludes

/03 Aug 3304/

[[id:1d726aa0-3e07-43b4-9b72-074046d25c3c][The Alliance]] has announced that its appeal for mined resources has reached a successful conclusion, having received an overwhelming response from the galactic community. Huge quantities of raw material were delivered to [[id:1d0d1587-a0be-4fff-b8b3-3ed448399620][California Gateway]] over the past week, which will be used to construct an Ocellus starport in the [[id:adb73fee-c5bc-4fb6-a4bd-bc120a6cb4cf][MEL 22 Sector GM-V c2-8]] system.

With the campaign at an end, Alliance official [[id:eb46eb3f-c399-45fa-a0e3-8362595e24ee][Torvan Dast]] issued the following statement:

“We are grateful for the efforts made by those pilots who helped bring this initiative to fruition. Construction of this starport will make the long journey to the [[id:6fee136e-381f-47a2-ba13-6d74c8798803][California Nebula]] safer for traders and colonists alike.”

Construction of the new starport is expected to begin soon. Pilots who contributed to the initiative can now collect their rewards from [[id:1d0d1587-a0be-4fff-b8b3-3ed448399620][California Gateway]] in the [[id:fd4456a5-20f9-45dc-9d7e-84114b4c251f][Synuefai EB-R c7-5]] system.
