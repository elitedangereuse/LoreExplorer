#+title: Finn McMillan
#+filetags: :3302:Player:Individual:

Finn McMillan is a Pilots Federation commander associated with the
Earth Expeditionary Fleet, the Formidine Rifters and the Cassiopeia
Project. He was quoted in multiple 3302 exploration-related reports.
