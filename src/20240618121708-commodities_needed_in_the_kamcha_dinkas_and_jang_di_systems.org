* Commodities needed in the Kamcha, Dinkas and Jang Di Systems

/20 Aug 3301/

Commodities needed in the Kamcha, Dinkas and Jang Di Systems

A mysterious organisation called Emperor’s Dawn has issued open contracts for a diverse selection of commodities: [[id:0ca21b16-f85b-4e7c-9389-6196d4f52ef1][Ceremonial Heike Tea]], [[id:6f8b14ec-06ee-4f36-b646-15146a0ca81b][Battle Weapons]], and [[id:4390832c-737a-482e-aabf-9b8595910797][Toxic Waste]]. Each of these is to be delivered to a specific starport, as outlined below:

[[id:6f8b14ec-06ee-4f36-b646-15146a0ca81b][Battle Weapons]] should be taken to Saarinen Station in the Kamcha system

[[id:0ca21b16-f85b-4e7c-9389-6196d4f52ef1][Ceremonial Heike Tea]] should be taken to Sikorsky Orbital in the Jang Di system

[[id:4390832c-737a-482e-aabf-9b8595910797][Toxic Waste]] should be taken to Blaauw Port in the Dinkas system

Little is known about Emperor’s Dawn, but it seems that the curiously-named organisation, which is based in the Kamcha system, has access to considerable resources, as it is throwing a lot of money at this commercial endeavour. Pilots of a mercantile disposition would do well to consider participating in this effort, as there is certainly an opportunity for resourceful traders to make a tidy profit.
