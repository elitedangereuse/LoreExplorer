#+title: Disappearances of the Far God Cultists
#+filetags: :3304:Federation:Thargoid:

In 3304, members of the [[id:04ae001b-eb07-4812-a42e-4bb72825609b][Far God]] cult vanished from hive-chapels and
outposts across multiple systems. Official explanations shifted from
suspicion of Thargoid links to the possibility that adherents had fled,
but later discoveries at Etain and survivor testimony left elements of
the mystery unresolved.
