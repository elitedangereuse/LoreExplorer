#+title: Major Faction
#+filetags: :manual:Federation:Empire:Alliance:

A major faction is one of the galaxy's three superpowers:
[[id:d56d0a6d-142a-4110-9c9a-235df02a99e0][The Federation]], [[id:77cf2f14-105e-4041-af04-1213f3e7383c][The Empire]], or
[[id:1d726aa0-3e07-43b4-9b72-074046d25c3c][The Alliance]]. The players guides describe major factions as
vast political powers controlling enormous regions of inhabited space.

Many [[id:43c9c067-4718-4d81-99eb-d73467c36937][Minor Faction]]s align themselves with a major faction and adopt
its laws and customs. In [[id:4ed9ac75-9ddc-4533-bc21-00a775fbc0ee][Powerplay]], powers sit between major and
minor factions: often aligned with a superpower, but still acting as
independent strategic actors.
