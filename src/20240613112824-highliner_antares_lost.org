* 0123  Highliner Antares Lost
[[id:56a590bf-081a-4483-b38f-0c6e3b30cd75][Luyten 205-128]]

In 3251 the Antares, a technologically revolutionary passenger liner designed and built by the Sirius Corporation, disappeared during its first hyperspace jump. In 3301 [[id:f0655b3a-aca9-488f-bdb3-c481a42db384][Li Yong-Rui]], CEO of Sirius Corporation's governmental division, launced a search for the ship. Sirius Corporation confirmed discovery of the ship's remains in 3302.
