#+title: Barnacles
#+filetags: :Thargoid:

Barnacles are non-human structures associated with the
[[id:09343513-2893-458e-a689-5865fdc32e0a][Thargoids]]. They are an important source of
[[id:da2f167d-0157-4deb-afb2-98bf6518cf01][Meta-Alloys]] and became a major focus of investigation in the
[[id:70fa34ea-bc98-40ff-97f0-e4f4538387a6][Merope]] and wider Pleiades region.
