#+title: Turmoil
#+filetags: :manual:Federation:Empire:Alliance:

Turmoil is the crisis state a power enters in [[id:4ed9ac75-9ddc-4533-bc21-00a775fbc0ee][Powerplay]] when it
begins a cycle with a deficit of [[id:ebcbe53a-2259-4e2b-b88c-f19f5877de33][Command Capital]]. The players guides
state that the [[id:1aaf6f29-ef6a-430c-b4e4-f40cf903a7a9][Control System]] with the highest upkeep cost falls
into turmoil first, followed by additional systems until the power
returns to a positive reserve.

Control systems already in turmoil stop drawing income from nearby
[[id:69d33069-1971-47bd-8aff-fd9d06dfcfc6][Exploited System]]s. If the power still has a CC deficit at the
end of the next cycle, those systems can revolt and cease to be
controlled.
