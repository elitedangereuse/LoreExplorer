#+title: Generation Ship Commission
#+filetags: :3305:Federation:Organization:

The Generation Ship Commission was a [[id:d56d0a6d-142a-4110-9c9a-235df02a99e0][Federal]] body founded in 3305 to recognise [[id:951f3d20-c3aa-41cc-ba58-cc7d3a5a1d07][generation ship]] colonists and support their reintegration into modern society if needed.

Its director at the time of foundation was [[id:59520cfa-c748-47b0-977e-244c6d5ba6c9][Cosima Fairfax]].
