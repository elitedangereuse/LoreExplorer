#+title: Imogen Gabrielini
#+filetags: :3276:Empire:Individual:

Imogen 'Eccentrica' Gabrielini was an entertainer, the spouse of
[[id:28fc3b99-d8a7-49e1-9593-1db6043430c7][Prince Harold Duval]], and the mother of Princess
[[id:b402bbe3-5119-4d94-87ee-0ba279658383][Aisling Duval]].
