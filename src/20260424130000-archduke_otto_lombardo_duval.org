#+title: Archduke Otto Lombardo-Duval
#+filetags: :3308:3309:Empire:Individual:

Archduke Otto Lombardo-Duval is a member of the Imperial Family. GalNet
described him as the son of Prince Baylor Duval, one of Emperor
[[id:8dc482e7-e978-4e6e-8a63-600e61cd715f][Hesketh]]'s brothers, whose branch married into the House of Lombardo.

By 3308 and 3309, Otto was counted among the surviving Duval relatives
given increased prominence after the NMLA assassinations. Contemporary
reports describe him as having multiple offspring and twelve
grandchildren.
