#+title: Operation HR 706
#+filetags: :3301:Empire:

Operation HR 706 was a follow-up Imperial initiative in 3301 aimed at
identifying who hired the would-be assassin who targeted Emperor
[[id:3cb0755e-4deb-442b-898b-3f0c6651636e][Hengist Duval]]. Reporting around the campaign later implicated leaders
of Emperor's Grace in the plot.
