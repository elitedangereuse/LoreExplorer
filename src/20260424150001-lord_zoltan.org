#+title: Lord Zoltan
#+filetags: :3301:3302:Player:Individual:

Lord Zoltan is a Pilots Federation commander repeatedly quoted in
Canonn-related freelance reports during 3301 and 3302. He was described
as "The Voice of Varati" and acted as a recurring public-facing source
for commentary on Professor Palin, barnacles and Dr Arcanonn's work.
