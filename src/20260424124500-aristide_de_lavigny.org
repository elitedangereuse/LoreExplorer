#+title: Aristide de Lavigny
#+filetags: :3250:Empire:Individual:

Prince Aristide de Lavigny was the husband of [[id:33f63de9-fd79-4790-a1a5-ebd87aaeea2d][Florence Lavigny]] and was
long believed to be the father of [[id:34f3cfdd-0536-40a9-8732-13bf3a5e4a70][Arissa Lavigny-Duval]].

Official reports later stated that in 3250 he met with a mysterious
accident that was never fully explained. Arissa's later claim to be the
daughter of Emperor [[id:3cb0755e-4deb-442b-898b-3f0c6651636e][Hengist Duval]] changed the understanding of his
place in the Imperial line, but not his role in her public upbringing.
