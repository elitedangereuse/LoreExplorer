#+title: Prince Harold Duval
#+filetags: :3230:3306:Empire:Individual:

Prince Harold Duval was the only known son and heir of [[id:3cb0755e-4deb-442b-898b-3f0c6651636e][Hengist Duval]].
Born in 3230, he later fell from grace and was declared unfit for the
throne, allowing his half-sister [[id:34f3cfdd-0536-40a9-8732-13bf3a5e4a70][Arissa Lavigny-Duval]] to succeed.

Harold married entertainer [[id:b41fca0e-d1b4-4a91-8a22-e45992982714][Imogen Gabrielini]], and their daughter was
Princess [[id:b402bbe3-5119-4d94-87ee-0ba279658383][Aisling Duval]]. In later life he became a recluse on
[[id:912f1172-e2b9-4fe9-9e53-13dd44fac4c4][Capitol]] and was assassinated by the [[id:dbfbb5eb-82a2-43c8-afb9-252b21b8464f][NMLA]] in 3306.
