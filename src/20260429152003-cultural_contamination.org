#+title: Cultural Contamination
#+filetags: :Federation:Law:

Cultural contamination refers to legal and ethical concerns around outsiders disrupting isolated micro-societies, including communities aboard [[id:951f3d20-c3aa-41cc-ba58-cc7d3a5a1d07][generation ships]] and [[id:eb7f4d60-9116-4d68-a0c2-13ac1e74d72e][dredger clans]].

The [[id:d56d0a6d-142a-4110-9c9a-235df02a99e0][Federation]] applied this principle to the [[id:fce1d147-f900-41ec-a92c-3ce3d1cae641][Golconda]] and [[id:d87d30e1-1624-4337-a252-64085c3c4c27][Forester's Choice]], treating the Golcondan people as a protected isolated society.
