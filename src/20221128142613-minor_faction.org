#+title: Minor Faction
#+filetags: :manual:Federation:Empire:Alliance:

A minor faction is a local political, corporate or ideological
authority that generally controls part or most of a single inhabited
system. The players guides distinguish minor factions from
[[id:ecd67dbd-9790-48f2-bfa3-710dde96c2d3][Major Faction]]s and from [[id:4ed9ac75-9ddc-4533-bc21-00a775fbc0ee][Powerplay]] powers.

Many minor factions align themselves with one of the major factions and
adopt its laws and customs, but they retain their own local
jurisdictions and reputations.
