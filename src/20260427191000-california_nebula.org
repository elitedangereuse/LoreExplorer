#+title: California Nebula

The California Nebula is a human frontier enclave associated with
[[id:09343513-2893-458e-a689-5865fdc32e0a][Thargoid]] [[id:b41c0ea3-93f4-4aef-8c9c-db0b140719c0][barnacle]] sites and Alliance-backed
research and colonisation efforts. During the 3300s it became linked to
the activities of the [[id:f76ba965-89ab-484f-8e67-b1996174a4a8][Turner Research Group]], the
[[id:5f78f661-9227-4f54-8a93-50052900119a][Spinward Marches Alliance Concern]] and, later, conflict involving the
[[id:7aae0550-b8ba-42cf-b52b-e7040461c96f][Kumo Crew]].
