* 0639 Hole in the Sky
[[id:00d0792d-a370-42ac-9afd-a83716c1449e][DRYUAE AOSCS IR-W E1-3926]]

Commander Sand dan Glokta recommends this site of celestial beauty; a black hole inside a dark blue planetary nebula, which is in turn inside a large, dense cluster of bright blue stars. This is unusual amongst planetary nebulae in that its central remnant has already captured some extrasolar planets.
