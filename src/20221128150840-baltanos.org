#+begin_quote
Hailing from a nation where technology is considered a gateway to
spiritual fulfillment, Baltanos serves his church in spreading its
transhumanist creed. Many cybernetic implants have altered - or
'blessed' - his ability to manipulate components facilitating a
successful engineering career. Eager to complete a long pilgrimmage
to humanity's furthest reaches, Baltanos now functions as a
missionary from his workshop in the Colonia region.
