#+title: East India Company
#+filetags: :Corporation:

The East India Company is an Imperial trade organisation active in
rare-goods trading, convoy protection, and the distribution of
[[id:b160f4b1-1751-41c2-b0bd-e7c6e8dc2f2d][Onionhead]]. It is headquartered in [[id:b634e896-9d3d-4f44-9783-cfb8fd6d0c56][Kappa Fornacis]], and Senator
[[id:d8e3667c-3ba1-43aa-bc90-dac719c6d5e7][Zemina Torval]] is described as a major shareholder.
