#+title: Disappearance of Olav Redcourt
#+filetags: :3304:

Author [[id:103de6dd-c4ec-4687-8b3c-24f57fa309f7][Olav Redcourt]] vanished during his 3304 promotional tour after
breaking with his publisher. He was later found alive at a remote
writers' retreat, making the episode a short-lived but widely
publicised mystery.
