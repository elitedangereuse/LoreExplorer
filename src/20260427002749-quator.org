#+title: Quator
#+filetags: :System:

#+begin_quote
A lawless backwater, despite the efforts of Wreaken Construction and
Mining. The Furnace battle group from Alioth under the command of
Admiral Bryce Jander were based here before they journeyed to Lave
to take part in the famous 'Battle of Lave Station' in 3265. Quator
remains a haven for pirates. 'Hoopy's Bar' on Quator Station should
not be confused with the popular casino chain of the 3200s.
#+end_quote
