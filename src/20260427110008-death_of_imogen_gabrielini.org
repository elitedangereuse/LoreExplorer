#+title: Death of Imogen Gabrielini
#+filetags: :3276:Empire:

[[id:b41fca0e-d1b4-4a91-8a22-e45992982714][Imogen Gabrielini]] died shortly after the birth of Princess
[[id:b402bbe3-5119-4d94-87ee-0ba279658383][Aisling Duval]]. The official autopsy cited an aneurysm brought on by an
overdose of Purple Sunflake, but rumours persisted that she had been
murdered to remove an embarrassment to the Royal family.
