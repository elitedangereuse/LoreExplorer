#+title: Augustus Sym
#+filetags: :3302:Empire:Individual:

Augustus Sym was described by GalNet as an Imperial veteran and former
test pilot based in the [[id:bed8c27f-3cbe-49ad-b86f-7d87eacf804a][Achenar]] system.

After the discovery of mysterious wreckage in 3302, Sym publicly stated
his belief that the remains were those of an alien ship.
