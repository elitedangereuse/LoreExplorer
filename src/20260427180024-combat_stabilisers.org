#+title: Combat Stabilisers
#+filetags: :Commodity:

#+begin_quote
Biological agents used to sustain and heal injured humans in combat.
Combat Stabilizers allow recipients to continue to function despite
unimaginable amounts of pain. Some cultures consider they encourage
glorification of combat, and so are illegal.
#+end_quote

Category: Medicines
