#+title: Undermining
#+filetags: :manual:Federation:Empire:Alliance:

Undermining is the process by which supporters of rival powers increase
the cost of holding a [[id:1aaf6f29-ef6a-430c-b4e4-f40cf903a7a9][Control System]] in
[[id:4ed9ac75-9ddc-4533-bc21-00a775fbc0ee][Powerplay]]. The players guides treat it as the
counterpart to [[id:4bd5f9da-c3b3-4f5f-b4de-bd668b992a63][Fortification]]: a temporary cycle-based action used to
destabilise a power's position and increase its
[[id:ebcbe53a-2259-4e2b-b88c-f19f5877de33][Command Capital]] burden.

Like fortification, undermining is cleared when the next Powerplay
cycle begins.
