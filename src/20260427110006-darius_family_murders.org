#+title: Darius Family Murders
#+filetags: :3301:Empire:

The immediate family of Emperor [[id:3cb0755e-4deb-442b-898b-3f0c6651636e][Hengist Duval]]'s assassin Brendan
Paul Darius were murdered in [[id:9fa174ce-7273-40ba-a0e6-1225bcda40b6][Eotienses]] in 3301. The investigation ended
in a firefight that killed the suspected vigilantes, and the case
helped Imperial investigators conclude that Darius had not acted alone.
