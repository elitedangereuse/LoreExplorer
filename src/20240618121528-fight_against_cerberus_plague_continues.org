* Fight Against Cerberus Plague Continues

/02 Aug 3301/

Fight Against Cerberus Plague Continues

The drive to deliver quantities of [[id:0ca21b16-f85b-4e7c-9389-6196d4f52ef1][Ceremonial Heike Tea]] to Hart Station in the [[id:ba9787a7-583f-4fb0-b9e1-3c0c0096b485][Bast]] System continues, as the starport’s medical teams work around the clock to create as much of the Cerberus Plague antidote as they can. According to [[id:f4d96510-b3f2-43e0-9627-73733c9c8ae2][Carl Simmons]], the Chief Medical Officer of Newton Dock, the rare tea is the only substance that can counter the virulent effects of the disease.

Independent pilots who are in a position to help with the effort are reminded that the Union Of [[id:ba9787a7-583f-4fb0-b9e1-3c0c0096b485][Bast]] Liberals is offering incentives to pilots who deliver [[id:0ca21b16-f85b-4e7c-9389-6196d4f52ef1][Ceremonial Heike Tea]], which can only be purchased in [[id:01253853-00a5-4182-b048-84d4735be964][Brunel City]] Station in Heike, to Hart Station.
