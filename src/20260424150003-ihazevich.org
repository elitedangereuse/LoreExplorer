#+title: Ihazevich
#+filetags: :3302:Player:Individual:

Ihazevich was one of the independent pilots who discovered the remains
of an alien wreck on Pleiades Sector AB-W B2-4 9 A in 3302. Later
references named the nearby Noctrach-Ihazevich Research Facility after
the two discoverers.
