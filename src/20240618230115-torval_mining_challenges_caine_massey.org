* Torval Mining Challenges Caine-Massey

/11 Jan 3308/

The company owned by Senator [[id:d8e3667c-3ba1-43aa-bc90-dac719c6d5e7][Zemina Torval]] is attempting to acquire a mining contract previously awarded to the [[id:87b860c8-570a-4c72-9524-93ba61af84a3][Caine-Massey]] corporation.

The agreement affects multiple corporations on the fringes of Federal space around the Dulos system, who for the last decade have purchased mined ore and other raw materials directly from [[id:87b860c8-570a-4c72-9524-93ba61af84a3][Caine-Massey]]. With the contract up for renewal, [[id:62ab96ef-615e-4fd5-a2b1-68167557baa1][Torval Mining Ltd]] has made an official bid.

An announcement was made by [[id:d50fdb9a-9f99-41e7-b831-8e347f851541][Constantia Torval]], the company’s operations director and daughter of [[id:d8e3667c-3ba1-43aa-bc90-dac719c6d5e7][Zemina Torval]]:

“We have enjoyed enormous success in a short space of time by providing services to the Empire. This is an opportunity to reach a broader customer base, and convince them that they too can benefit from [[id:62ab96ef-615e-4fd5-a2b1-68167557baa1][Torval Mining]]’s expertise.”

[[id:8e322aa9-b969-49a4-be27-339e3c107d10][Johann Fleischer]], the vice president of [[id:87b860c8-570a-4c72-9524-93ba61af84a3][Caine-Massey]], remarked:

“Our corporation is proud of its origins within the Federation, and we have supplied these systems with resources for many years. This is a surreptitious attempt by an Imperial senator to boost her own standing by hijacking Federal business.”

The Federal Commerce Authority has responded to [[id:87b860c8-570a-4c72-9524-93ba61af84a3][Caine-Massey]]’s request for intervention. An initial review found that, while it recognises the aggression of the [[id:62ab96ef-615e-4fd5-a2b1-68167557baa1][Torval Mining]] claims, there is no exclusivity clause in place that prevents the companies in question from holding talks with an Imperial supplier now [[id:87b860c8-570a-4c72-9524-93ba61af84a3][Caine-Massey]]’s contract term is expiring.
