#+title: Thargoid Sensor
#+filetags: :Commodity:

#+begin_quote
These strange objects are of Thargoid origin. They have the ability
to repair themselves by extracting non-organic material from their
immediate environment, and this self-repair mechanism can severely
damage starships and other machinery. They can also scan nearby
vessels and celestial bodies, and transmit information about the
scanned object in an encrypted form.
#+end_quote
