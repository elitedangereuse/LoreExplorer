#+title: Imperial Investigation into Attack on Emperor Hengist Duval
#+filetags: :3301:Empire:

After an attempted assassination of Emperor [[id:3cb0755e-4deb-442b-898b-3f0c6651636e][Hengist Duval]] in 3301,
Princess [[id:34f3cfdd-0536-40a9-8732-13bf3a5e4a70][Arissa Lavigny-Duval]] and Chancellor [[id:e9679720-e0c1-449e-86a6-a5b3de3613f5][Anders Blaine]] pursued
parallel investigations. The campaign led to anti-crime operations in
[[id:1b91efee-b411-45a9-8b03-df967281885d][Nehet]] and [[id:c72ce3b7-f19e-4034-9df5-554bcddfaa4f][Mictlan]], and later to allegations against Emperor's
Grace in HR 706.

See also [[id:9f0d8885-7d6d-48e6-8c17-1140294577ff][Operation Nehet]], [[id:3af1dab3-deb5-4756-9a03-a2f86f409d6c][Operation Mictlan]] and [[id:9d17364e-00f0-43a2-8b5f-3d082b47742a][Operation HR 706]].
