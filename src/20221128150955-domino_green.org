relations initiative by the Rockforth Corporation; the company
housed and educated her, but expected her to work for Rockforth as
an adult. While displaying a prodigious talent for technology, she
also lacked discipline and proved highly irreverent. She was
dismissed as a poor investment and now operates a workshop
established with her family inheritance.
