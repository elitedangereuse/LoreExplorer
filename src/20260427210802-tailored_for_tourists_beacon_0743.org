* 0743 Tailored for Tourists
[[id:b5dd3158-fb0c-4ccc-8fe0-ec2d3ae9c634][NGC 7822 Sector FW-W c1-1]]

This tourist beacon was set up in September 3307 as part of the [[id:49b21fdc-6a96-42b3-a496-123e8106f102][Saud Kruger]] Exploration Campaign in conjunction with Astrogator Tours. The Location was identified by Commander filthEfrank.

Both [[id:49b21fdc-6a96-42b3-a496-123e8106f102][Saud Kruger]] and Astrogator Tours extend their thanks to the Commander filthEfrank for contributing to the success of this initiative.
