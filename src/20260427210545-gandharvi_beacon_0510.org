* 0510 Gandharvi
[[id:fb3c8beb-2697-42c0-8476-588d747ff061][Gandharvi]]

Commander [[id:97f03336-3fcf-4e66-a1fc-7ad80bfd13c9][Edgar Starwalker]] suggested visiting this [[id:7fec1142-ab20-47e1-b746-36edee889cc3][Earth-like]] world discovered during the Chapterhouse's August 3302 Repair mission to [[id:f37f17f1-8eb3-4598-93f7-190fe97438a1][Jaques]]. The group hopes that this world and system could one day play host to a stepping stone base towards [[id:ba6c6359-137b-4f86-ad93-f8ae56b0ad34][Colonia]], as well as a staging ground for wider Imperial exploration efforts of this region of the Sctutum-Crux Arm. Gandharvi is another of the children of [[id:6762f716-7e24-4a03-af96-2af0fd55f8c7][Kamadhenu]] in Hindu mythology.
