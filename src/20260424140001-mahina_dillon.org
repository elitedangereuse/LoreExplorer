#+title: Mahina Dillon
#+filetags: :3302:Individual:

Mahina Dillon was a gambler associated with the Granger Gang. In 3302
she was one of the figures tied to the public clue hunt that led to the
discovery of mysterious wreckage in the [[id:c32901ed-73d1-4ca6-aeb8-5bcd795d1036][Pleiades Sector AB-W B2-4]]
system.

An official community goal described Dillon as demanding enough tobacco
"to last a lifetime" before releasing further information.
