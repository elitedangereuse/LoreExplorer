#+begin_quote
Rhenium is a silvery-white heavy transition metal. Its atomic number
is 75 and was considered one of the rarest metals on Earth. However
through the ages of galactic expansion more plentiful sources of the
element have been discovered and it is regularly found when
extracting other ores from asteroids.

Rhenium is used in combustion chambers, turbine blades and exhaust
systems.
#+end_quote
