be the illegitimate child of an Imperial Guard officer. Her own
military career was cut short when the circumstances surrounding her
birth were revealed, resulting in her expulsion from the guard. Many
in her unit demonstrated their loyalty by joining her once they'd
complete their service, attracted to the comfortable living Tiana is
able to offer them.
