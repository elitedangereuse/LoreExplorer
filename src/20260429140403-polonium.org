Polonium, Po, atomic number 84. Melting point 527K. Polonium is an
intense source of alpha particles and in sufficient quantities will
generate enough heat for radioisotope thermoelectric generators.
This technology is ancient by modern standards, but the properties
are still useful for certain applications.
