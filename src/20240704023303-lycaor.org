The Lycaor was a [[id:951f3d20-c3aa-41cc-ba58-cc7d3a5a1d07][generation ship]] discovered in the [[id:448469f4-f4a5-4cdb-80d9-d040393740bf][Alaunus]] system after a transmission was recovered from a [[id:4df4a36a-20bf-43ca-9bf1-2324f832ab81][Listening Post]] in the [[id:079cf648-49db-4fb6-825b-e27e68ccf2ef][LHS 1047]] system.

Its crew was killed by an unidentified pathogen.
