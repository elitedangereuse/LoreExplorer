#+title: Heavy Combat in Chi Eridani Subsides
#+filetags: :galnet:

/10 Apr 3311/

A proxy conflict between supporters of [[id:7bdfd887-d1db-46bc-98c4-2fb39bfcc914][Jerome Archer]] and [[id:75daea85-5e9f-4f6f-a102-1a5edea0283c][Denton
Patreus]] appears to be slowing down after several days of sustained
fighting.

Battlefield reporters for the [[id:b05e910f-52f3-4c2b-b9c6-0f4feba31d5b][Chi Eridani Herald]] state that the
[[id:7bdfd887-d1db-46bc-98c4-2fb39bfcc914][Jerome Archer]] group emerged victorious, scoring a higher number of
confirmed victories in the area.

A spokesperson for [[id:7bdfd887-d1db-46bc-98c4-2fb39bfcc914][Jerome Archer]] provided the following to the Herald:

“It's obvious that this outcome proves the strong influence and
military might of [[id:7bdfd887-d1db-46bc-98c4-2fb39bfcc914][Jerome Archer]]’s supporters in [[id:a00defdc-ae6a-4baf-9a13-3f042e63ef47][Chi Eridani]]. Hopefully
this leads to a widespread acceptance of the benefits and protection
that Archer’s leadership can bring to the system.”

Those who took part in the conflict for either side can now collect their rewards.

[[id:7bdfd887-d1db-46bc-98c4-2fb39bfcc914][Jerome Archer]] supporters can collect their rewards at [[id:87559b48-960d-4360-922d-c3ecdbb6fc67][Steve Masters]]
starport.

[[id:75daea85-5e9f-4f6f-a102-1a5edea0283c][Denton Patreus]] supporters can collect their rewards at the
[[id:eecdd0bd-edad-440d-8e28-bf821cac4227][Evanescent Dusk]] megaship.
