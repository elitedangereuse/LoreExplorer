* 0014 Neutron Earth-Like
[[id:5fddf317-26ea-40ba-af38-86c29b47d533][BLAE EAEC PM-W E1-205]]

This ringed [[id:7fec1142-ab20-47e1-b746-36edee889cc3][Earth-like]] planet is the only body rotating around this neutron star. Commander Dragongard states that because it is hard to find, it's one of the few places humanity could hide if there was ever a danger of extinction.
