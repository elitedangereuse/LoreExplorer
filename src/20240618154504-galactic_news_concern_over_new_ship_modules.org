* Galactic News: Concern over New Ship Modules

/01 Apr 3302/

Recent reports indicate that some members of the galactic community are concerned about the development of Unknown Artefact-resistant ship modules. Critics of the new technology, developed by Professor [[id:36896a71-f032-45ee-a6da-ec7d7216d493][Lea Tantaga]], have claimed it will facilitate further black-market trade of Unknown Artefacts, thus exacerbating the so-called [[id:002fd942-f71a-4d8f-a431-4ad26857c771][technological plague]].

In an attempt to counter the rising concern, a member of the technical team at [[id:4223b99a-7e0c-44fa-b7e9-afeca7e0c031][Obsidian Orbital]] released the following statement:

"I know some people are concerned that these new modules might aggravate the [[id:002fd942-f71a-4d8f-a431-4ad26857c771][technological plague]], but there's every reason to believe we can use the Unknown Artefact-resistant material developed by Professor Tantaga to protect our starports from UA-related malfunctions. All that's needed is for pilots to deliver meta-alloys to the affected starports. We'll do the rest."
