#+title: Prince Baylor Duval
#+filetags: :3309:Empire:Individual:

Prince Baylor Duval was one of Emperor [[id:8dc482e7-e978-4e6e-8a63-600e61cd715f][Hesketh]]'s brothers and the
father of [[id:2a0cde1a-b7e0-48fb-840a-19e70e1043f3][Archduke Otto Lombardo-Duval]].

GalNet reported that Baylor's branch of the Imperial Family married
into the House of Lombardo nearly a century ago, forming the
Lombardo-Duval line.
