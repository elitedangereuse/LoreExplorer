* Galactic News: California Campaign Concludes

/17 Nov 3303/

[[id:7d70f017-bc97-4ded-894c-9330444818d6][Civility Marks]], the Chief Security Officer for the [[id:5f78f661-9227-4f54-8a93-50052900119a][Spinward Marches Alliance Concern]], has announced that the organisation’s campaign to establish an agricultural facility in the [[id:a77e0244-1410-4740-907a-c49f0b8f083f][Synuefai LX-R d5-28]] system has reached a successful conclusion, thanks to support from the space-faring community. Construction of the facility is expected to begin within the next few weeks. 

The [[id:5f78f661-9227-4f54-8a93-50052900119a][Spinward Marches Alliance Concern]] has extended its gratitude to those who supported the initiative, and invited them to try Morrina or [[id:6b34e656-2e39-4920-9760-57a270e07fa2][Badfort Tea]] when returning to [[id:b811183a-04cf-4ca1-9674-2b8da88ee0d6][Hansford’s Landing]]. 

Pilots who contributed to the initiative can now collect their rewards from [[id:b811183a-04cf-4ca1-9674-2b8da88ee0d6][Hansford’s Landing]] in the [[id:9bd5cda6-b87e-424e-9e84-e27c742c86ad][BD+22 4939]] system.
