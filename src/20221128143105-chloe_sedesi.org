#+begin_quote
Chloe Sedesi served a number of artificer apprenticeships before
joining Professor Ishmael Palin's staff as a mechanic. She swiftly
became a vital team member, displaying a remarkable aptitude for a
variety of technical disciplines. When the Witch Head enclave was
established, Chloe resigned from Palin's team and, with his
blessing, traveled to Cinder Dock to offer the techniques she had
learned at the Palin Research Institute.
