#+title: Nanobreakers

#+begin_quote
Used by factions as a reward for mission contracts.

Nanobreakers are tiny machines used for disassembling certain,
specialised materials. While they can be sold at markets for a
minimal price, these are more often used by engineers for their
upgrades.
