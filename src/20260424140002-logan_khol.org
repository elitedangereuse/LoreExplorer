#+title: Logan Khol
#+filetags: :3302:Individual:

Logan Khol was a smuggler associated with the Granger Gang. In 3302 he
was one of the figures tied to the public clue hunt that led to the
discovery of mysterious wreckage in the [[id:c32901ed-73d1-4ca6-aeb8-5bcd795d1036][Pleiades Sector AB-W B2-4]]
system.

GalNet reported that Khol released one of the clues after his request
for narcotics was met. The associated community goal used the spelling
"Logan Kohl".
