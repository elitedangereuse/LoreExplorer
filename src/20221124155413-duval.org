#+title: Duval Family
#+filetags: :Empire:

The Duval Family, or House of Duval, is the ruling dynasty of the
Empire and the core bloodline within the wider [[id:85e6fd5f-69a0-4f7c-95d0-ddb89ef150e9][Imperial Family]]. Modern GalNet reporting still describes Imperial politics in
terms of the Duval bloodline, the Duval dynasty and the House of Duval,
especially whenever questions of succession, legitimacy or family unity
come to the fore.

The formal ruling line is summarised in [[id:98b44118-c603-4868-ad86-a65c188f589d][The Line of the Duvals]]. In the
modern period, the most important transition was from [[id:8dc482e7-e978-4e6e-8a63-600e61cd715f][Hesketh Duval]] to
[[id:3cb0755e-4deb-442b-898b-3f0c6651636e][Hengist Duval]], and then to [[id:34f3cfdd-0536-40a9-8732-13bf3a5e4a70][Arissa Lavigny-Duval]] after Hengist’s
assassination in 3301.

* Modern family branches

The present-day family is no longer just the direct imperial line.
GalNet coverage of the 3300s regularly refers to several connected
branches whose standing changed after the NMLA assassinations and the
renewed focus on succession.

** Arissa and the Lavigny branch

- [[id:34f3cfdd-0536-40a9-8732-13bf3a5e4a70][Arissa Lavigny-Duval]]
- [[id:33f63de9-fd79-4790-a1a5-ebd87aaeea2d][Florence Lavigny]]
- [[id:207bef41-d5d6-434d-b56d-acf153e59896][Aristide de Lavigny]]

Arissa was long believed to be the daughter of [[id:207bef41-d5d6-434d-b56d-acf153e59896][Aristide de Lavigny]], and
therefore part of the family through the Lavigny marriage. Her claim to
be Hengist’s daughter was later ratified, placing her in the direct
imperial line while leaving the Lavigny connection politically
important.

** Harold and Aisling

- [[id:28fc3b99-d8a7-49e1-9593-1db6043430c7][Prince Harold Duval]]
- [[id:b402bbe3-5119-4d94-87ee-0ba279658383][Aisling Duval]]

Harold was Hengist’s only known son and heir, but his exclusion from
the succession weakened this branch. Even so, Aisling remained a viable
public contender after Hengist’s death, and she still represents one of
the dynasty’s most visible modern figures.

** Hadrian and his descendants

- [[id:c4f47591-9c52-441f-8853-536f577de922][Hadrian Augustus Duval]]
- [[id:ef9ddb06-8cb2-4c3f-a688-469be3149aa9][Astrid Minerva-Duval]]
- [[id:c51145ab-32ee-42ec-a884-bbbc263b06dd][Hector Mordanticus Duval]]

Hadrian emerged as a newly recognised Duval descendant through an
illegitimate line descending from Hengist. Later reporting treated the
birth of Hector as an event of dynastic importance, since the child
might one day figure in the question of succession.

** Collateral Duval branches

- [[id:2a0cde1a-b7e0-48fb-840a-19e70e1043f3][Archduke Otto Lombardo-Duval]]
- [[id:3198b73e-a63a-4edd-aadd-d6cdbf197115][Lucretia Roche-Duval]]

These collateral branches descend from the siblings of Emperor Hesketh.
By 3308 and 3309 they were receiving more attention in GalNet coverage
as surviving members of the wider dynasty.

Together these notes show the central tension around the family in the
current era: Arissa is the reigning Emperor, but the dynasty’s future is
shaped by multiple surviving branches, contested legitimacy, and the
need to preserve a bloodline that many Imperials still regard as the
foundation of the state.
