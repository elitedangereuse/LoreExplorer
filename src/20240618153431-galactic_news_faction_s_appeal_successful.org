* Galactic News: Faction's Appeal Successful

/21 Jan 3302/

According a statement from the Green Party of [[id:eec7641e-126b-4c25-8f77-6fbc7ac5f096][HIP 112974]], the faction's recent appeal for onionhead has been successful. In the statement, a spokesperson confirmed the faction had received sufficient quantities of the drug to establish a new onionhead-trading operation:

"We're hugely grateful to all the traders who made deliveries to [[id:0ee160ba-8ffc-4d92-b499-c31066902715][la Cosa City]], and all the pilots who kept the traders safe by clearing the agitators from our spaceways. May this be the start of a long a profitable relationship between the Green Party of [[id:eec7641e-126b-4c25-8f77-6fbc7ac5f096][HIP 112974]] and the galactic community."

The Green Party of [[id:eec7641e-126b-4c25-8f77-6fbc7ac5f096][HIP 112974]] has confirmed that those who contributed to the appeal could collect their rewards from [[id:0ee160ba-8ffc-4d92-b499-c31066902715][la Cosa City]] in [[id:eec7641e-126b-4c25-8f77-6fbc7ac5f096][HIP 112974]].
