* Galactic News: Services Suspended at Fozard Dock

/18 Apr 3302/

Authorities at Fozard Dock in the HIP 19934 system have announced the immediate suspension of all services due to Unknown Artefact-related interference. Starport personnel have confirmed that only docking and shipyard access remain available. This brings the total number of starports closed as a result of the [[id:002fd942-f71a-4d8f-a431-4ad26857c771][technological plague]] to 12. The affected stations are:

Fozard Dock, HIP 19934

Ackerman Market, Eravate

Haack [[id:e179ecca-9ab3-4184-b05e-107b2e6932c2][Gateway]], Halbangaay

Lobachevsky Outpost, Pic Tok

Carter Port, [[id:6da9023a-ccb6-444a-be77-626dfb552eb1][Rhea]]
