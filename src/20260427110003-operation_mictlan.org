#+title: Operation Mictlan
#+filetags: :3301:Empire:

Operation Mictlan was a community initiative ordered by Princess
[[id:34f3cfdd-0536-40a9-8732-13bf3a5e4a70][Arissa Lavigny-Duval]] as part of the investigation into the attempted
assassination of Emperor [[id:3cb0755e-4deb-442b-898b-3f0c6651636e][Hengist Duval]]. Pilots targeted criminal
activity around the [[id:c72ce3b7-f19e-4034-9df5-554bcddfaa4f][Mictlan]] Cartel, and the resulting intelligence
helped connect the Silver Allied Network to the plot.
