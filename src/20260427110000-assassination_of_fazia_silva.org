#+title: Assassination of Fazia Silva
#+filetags: :3304:Alliance:

Alliance presidential candidate [[id:e46779af-a26e-45fb-a784-21e970eeaae1][Fazia Silva]] was murdered during the
3304 election campaign. Alliance Interpol later ruled the case a
homicide involving a sophisticated nerve toxin and probable
professional assassins, but no convictions were made.
