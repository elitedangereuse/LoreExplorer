* Merger Creates Mega-Corporation

/05 Dec 3304/

Two eminent corporations have merged to form one of the largest pharmaceutical companies in the galaxy.

Allied Medical Industries, based in [[id:1d726aa0-3e07-43b4-9b72-074046d25c3c][Alliance]] space, has united with Neosalve Inc., a conglomerate that supplies medicines to a great many independent systems.

The new mega-corporation, [[id:d8c4bbec-5bea-43d7-bad6-0ec97600cac3][Neomedical Industries]], will sell a drugs, equipment and services to both Alliance and independent systems.

The merger was closely monitored by the [[id:50320afd-f184-4f90-a94b-802400bb39a8][Interstellar Health Organisation]] (IHO), an official inter-superpower body that regulates developments in medical science.

In the pharmaceutical arena, only the Vandermeer Corporation, which supplies medicines exclusively within [[id:d56d0a6d-142a-4110-9c9a-235df02a99e0][Federal]] territory, is of a comparable size to the newly formed [[id:d8c4bbec-5bea-43d7-bad6-0ec97600cac3][Neomedical Industries]].
